About Detective Marllowe

Detective Scratches Marllowe was born and raised in Faunaville, Illinois. Having received apt training in the art of investigation, espionage, and criminology, he is well equipped to manuever stealthily through the city streets undetected. In five short years, he has investigated missing persons, retrieved lost items, exposed ongoing white collar crime rings, and infidelity cases. Two years prior, Detective Marllowe was notably involved in exposing a large scale corporate bust involving the transportation of uncut diamonds worth millions. While the exact wherabouts of the diamonds are still unknown, the perpetrators are currently behind bars.

If you are looking for someone who will stop at nothing to and isn't afraid to work in the shadows to do so, you have come to the right man.
